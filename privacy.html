Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection & Usage

NirviSha.ai collects financial metadata strictly for the purpose of facilitating Chartered Accountant (CA) automation workflows. This includes receipts, bank statements, and GSTINs submitted by your end-clients via our WhatsApp API.

2. Enterprise Confidentiality

Your data is completely isolated. NirviSha.ai employs strict row-level security protocols ensuring that financial data uploaded by one CA firm is cryptographically invisible to any other firm utilizing our platform.

3. AI Processing

NirviSha utilizes large language models (such as Google Gemini) strictly for structured data extraction (OCR). Client data passed through these APIs is ephemeral and is NOT utilized by underlying foundation models for permanent machine learning training.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or firm data. We only share necessary routing data with certified third-party infrastructure providers (e.g., Supabase, Vercel, Infobip) responsible for strictly hosting, database provision, and message delivery on behalf of NirviSha.ai.

5. Data Deletion

CA Administrators maintain full rights to request complete and irreversible deletion of their tenant data from our Supabase clusters by contacting our support team directly.
