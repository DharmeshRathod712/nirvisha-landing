FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Does my client need to install anything?
No. Clients send photos and documents on the same WhatsApp they already use — nothing to install, no registration, no training required. If your client can send a WhatsApp message, they can use NirviSha.
Does it work with Tally Prime and Tally ERP 9?
Yes. NirviSha exports standard Tally XML that imports seamlessly into both Tally Prime and Tally ERP 9. No custom Tally configuration needed.
Is each firm's data private and secure?
Absolutely. Each CA firm has completely isolated data on NirviSha. Your clients' bills and financial data are never visible to any other firm — ever. We use enterprise-grade security with row-level database isolation.
Can I add my junior staff?
Yes. You add staff accounts and control exactly what each staff member can see and do. Staff can be limited to specific clients. Only you (the CA admin) can approve, edit, or export.
How is NirviSha different from VouchRit or TaxOne?
NirviSha is the only tool where your clients themselves can upload documents directly via WhatsApp — no portal, no app, no staff involvement needed. VouchRit has no WhatsApp. TaxOne requires staff to upload on behalf of clients. Our approach eliminates the collection bottleneck entirely.
